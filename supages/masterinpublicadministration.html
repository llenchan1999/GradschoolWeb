MASTER IN PUBLIC ADMINISTRATION

PROGRAM OUTCOMES

The graduates of the program shall be able to have:
a. Advanced knowledge and skills in specialized, interdisciplinary, or multi-disciplinary field of study for professional education.
b. Self-directed research:
c. Lifelong learning with a highly substantial degree of independence that involves individual work or teams of interdisciplinary or multi-disciplinary; and
d. Application of the above-mentioned skills in research, professional, or creative work.
