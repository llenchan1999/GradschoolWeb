ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

Applicants must have a bachelor's degree from a recognized institution for those who wish to pursue a master's program, and a master's degree to pursue a doctoral program. He/She must

a. qualify in the admission examination; and
b. pass the interview by the program chair and/ or Dean;

The following admission requirements in the Graduate Programs must be submitted:

a. Transcript of Records from the last school attended
b. PSA issued Birth Certificate
c. Two (2) copies of most recent 2x2 ID picture (in business formal attire, white background)
d. Research concept ntpe
f. Recommendation from supervisor and former professor

Prepare scanned copy of the following documents when applying for admission:

1. 2x2 picture with white background (in JPEG format)
2. Transcript of Records (in JPEG or [PDF format)
3. PSA Birth Certificate (in JPEG or PDF format)
