GRADUATE SCHOOL GOALS

The Graduate School Prepares competent professional leaders who commit themselves to effect positive changes to the community and responsive inj providing efficient and excellent leadership in private and public sectors.

MARINDUQUE STATE COLLEGE MISSION

To provide excellence in instruction, research, extension, and production that magnifies W.I.S.D.O.M. in leadership through a total quality management system responsive to the challenges of 21th century education.

MARINDUQUE STATE COLLEGE VISION

An advanced and adaptive university pursuing quality education, lifelong gender-sensitive learning environment, responsive research-based community programs, and transparent governance with sustainable resource generation by 2025.
